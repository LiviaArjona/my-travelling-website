FRANCE

Paris

Paris, the enchanting "City of Light," is a timeless symbol of romance, culture, and elegance. With its iconic landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame Cathedral, picturesque streets, and world-class museums like the Louvre, Paris captivates visitors with its charm and sophistication. Renowned for its culinary delights, haute couture fashion, and vibrant street life, Paris offers a rich tapestry of experiences for travelers to explore. From strolling along the Seine River to savoring croissants in quaint cafes, every moment in Paris is imbued with a sense of magic and allure.

  • Main tourist areas that I liked the most:
    • 1. Eiffel Tower and Champ de Mars: The quintessential symbol of Paris, offering panoramic views of the city from its observation decks. Surrounding Champ de Mars park is perfect for picnics and leisurely strolls.
    • 2. Louvre Museum and Palais Royal: Home to thousands of works of art, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. The adjacent Palais Royal features elegant gardens and the iconic glass pyramid entrance.
    • 3. Notre-Dame Cathedral and Île de la Cité: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture, known for its stunning rose windows and gargoyles. Located on the historic Île de la Cité, surrounded by charming cafes and bookshops.
  • The average daily spend for a tourist in Paris
    • Accommodation: €50 - €100 (Hostels or budget hotels)
    • Food: €20 - €40 (Eating at bakeries, street food vendors, or budget-friendly cafes)
    • Transportation: €5 - €10 (Using public transportation, such as the metro or buses)
    • Activities: €10 - €20 (Visiting free attractions, self-guided walking tours, or enjoying public parks)
  • Total: €85 - €170 per day
